Description

This is an open discussion group, where we don't avoid the "hard" topics. In fact...We welcome them!

The goal of this group is to build a place of comfort and trust for those starting life after High School and entering early adulthood, to be able to come and talk openly about anything they may have questions about or struggles they may be facing. The group is led by 3 individuals, each with different personalities and gifts. Having these 3 leaders gives us the ability to commit one on one time to someone if they would like to speak about something privately, as well as provide different insights during our discussions.

If you are just getting out into the "real" world, and would like to join a group with others going through similar situations, please come see us or send us an email.
